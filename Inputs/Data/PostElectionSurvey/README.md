### Description

- `NewsDatabase.csv` contains information on news articles we use in the survey.
  - `Article Type` and `Article True/False` record our classification of the article.
  - `Article Text` and `Short Text` record the content of the article.
  - `Pro-Clinton or pro-Trump` records whether the article favors Clinton or Trump.
  - `Source`, `Fact check link`, `Coverage link 1` and `Coverage link 2` record the media coverage of the article.
  - `topicid` is used to merge with Facebook share dataset (`source/raw/FakeNews/FB shares - combined.csv`).

- `Sheet_1.csv` is the complete result of our survey.

- `ACS_14_5YR_B19013_with_ann.csv` is zip level median income downloaded from [http://factfinder.census.gov/](http://factfinder.census.gov/).

- `National population means for weighting.xlsx` contains the national weights we use in analyzing the survey to make the respondents national representative. The sources of the weights are recoded in the file.
